The Rules

Auctions are open to AUSTRALIAN RESIDENTS ONLY, if you don’t live in Australia you can not take part to the biddings neither you can be a member of this group

When you place a bid you agree to pay that amount in AUD in case your bid ends up to be the highest bid.

Winners must contact the seller within 24hrs from the auction end time and make the payment within 48hrs from the end of the auction. If a member refuses to pay for an item won at auction, the member WILL BE BANNED FROM POND.

Any dispute between sellers and buyers has to be dealt with in private.

Sellers must clearly specify method and cost of shipping and handling in the auction description

Seller can not modify or delete an auction if a valid bid has been placed on it. In the case a seller wants or needs to modify an auction that has already got a valid bid, the seller must contact admin first for authorisation.

Seller must use at least 1 picture of the item for sale. The PICTURE MUST BE OF THE ACTUAL ITEM FOR SALE, no google images or such

Seller must specify if moss is been grown emersed or submerged.

No buy it now option, pure auctions only

Bids has to be made in form of public comment with the number of AUD bidded Example: “$30 please” (VALID BID); “Thirty” (INVALID BID)


In case a reserve price is been asked by the seller, that price is the starting price of the auction, no bids are to be considered valid under the reserve price amount.

The winning bid is the last higher one placed the last second before the auction end. Example: if an auction is set to end at 8pm the last valid bid will be the higher one at 7:59:59 pm. In case of two bids of the same amount placed at the same time, the earliest bid wins

Abusive language or behaviour is not and will not be tollerated. Members using abusive behaviour or language WILL BE BANNED FROM THE GROUP.

To avoid confusion with other auctions groups all end times will have to be set to Sydney time

NO FOR SALE POSTS unless authorised by admin in writing

NO LINKS TO OTHER GROUPS OR PAGES unless authorised by admin in writing


Being a member of POND means you accept these rules. Breaking any of these rules will potentially end up with the member being banned from the page
